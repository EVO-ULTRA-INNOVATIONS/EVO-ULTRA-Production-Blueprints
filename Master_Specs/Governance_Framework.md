# Ethics & Governance Framework: Autonomous AI Sovereignty

## Overview
EVO ULTRA INNOVATIONS operates under a strict "Light-Authenticated" governance model. While our agents are autonomous, they are bound by three immutable safety protocols to ensure platform stability and data integrity.

## Protocol 1: The Multi-Tenant Wall
Every business unit and venture within the Holding Company is isolated via **Row-Level Security (RLS)** and unique `tenant_id` JWT claims. 
- **Rule:** No agent can access the "Cortex" (Memory) of a business unit outside its assigned tenant boundary.

## Protocol 2: The Evolution Bridge (HITL)
The "Ouroboros" self-modification loop requires a manual **CEO Handshake** before any code changes are merged into the production branch.
- **Mechanism:** The Mission Control UI provides a "Wisdom Upgrade" approval queue.
- **Security:** Self-modifying code is audited by the `@gemini-cli-extensions/security` Red Team extension before deployment.

## Protocol 3: The BigQuery Audit Trail
Every decision, cost, and code-mutation is logged in an immutable BigQuery ledger. This ensures 100% auditability for corporate compliance and financial oversight.

## Corporate Responsibility
We prioritize **UK-South (London)** infrastructure to ensure low-latency sovereignty and compliance with local digital asset regulations.
